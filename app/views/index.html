Make a money claim online

{{releaseVersion | log }}

You can make a money claim if you think a person or organisation owes you money and will not pay you back. You’ll have to pay a fee.

Before making a claim, contact the person or organisation to try to resolve the issue by discussion or mediation.

You cannot make a claim if you’re told that the person owing you money has protection from creditors through the ‘Breathing Space’ scheme. Find out what to do when someone is using the ‘Breathing Space’ scheme.

Your claim, including your name and address, will be sent to the person you say owes you money. They’ll get a chance to respond to your claim.

To make a money claim in Welsh, download and fill in form N1.
